Low Energy & Lethargy

If your dog seems constantly tired, lacks enthusiasm for walks, or sleeps more than usual, their diet might be the culprit.

Dogs need a balanced mix of proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates to maintain their energy levels throughout the day.

A diet lacking in essential nutrients can leave your pup feeling drained and unmotivated — even if they're eating enough calories.
💡 Look for foods with real meat as the first ingredient and avoid fillers like corn syrup or artificial preservatives.
