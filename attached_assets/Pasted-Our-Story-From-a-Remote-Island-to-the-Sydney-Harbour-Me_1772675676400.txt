Our Story: From a Remote Island to the Sydney Harbour
Meet Guk-dung: The Heart Behind SpoiltDogs
Every great story starts with a chance encounter. Ours began in 2017, on a small, windswept island in South Korea called Heuksandow.
There lived a semi-wild dog—strong, resilient, but lonely. When she gave birth to a litter of pups in the rugged outdoors, fate brought us together. Among those tiny fluffballs was Guk-dung, a proud Jindo dog who was born into the wild but destined for a life of love.
The Jindo Legacy
The Jindo is Korea’s National Treasure, known for their unwavering loyalty and intelligence. Yet, many of them back home live a contrasting reality—spending their lives at the end of a short chain or surviving as strays in rural villages.
Having seen Guk-dung’s mother struggle, I made a promise: to give this boy the world. I wanted to show that every dog, regardless of their beginning, deserves to be "spoilt" with the very best.
A New Chapter in Sydney
In 2019, Guk-dung and I packed our bags and flew across the ocean to start our new life in Sydney. From the quiet hills of Korea to the sunny parks of Australia, Guk-dung has been my constant shadow.
Caring for him opened my eyes to a whole new world. I became obsessed with finding the healthiest treats, the most comfortable harnesses, and toys that actually engaged his brilliant mind. I didn't just want him to survive; I wanted him to thrive.
Why We Started SpoiltDogs
Guk-dung is more than just a pet; he is the reason SpoiltDogs exists.
Watching him transform from a rescued pup into a happy, adventurous Sydney dog made me realize that every dog deserves that same "extra" level of care. We believe that being "spoilt" isn't about excess—it's about the deep, intentional love we show our furry family members every single day.
At SpoiltDogs, we curate everything with Guk-dung’s high standards in mind. We are here for the owners who, like us, see their dogs not just as animals, but as soulmates who deserve the finest life has to offer.
Because every dog has a story. Let’s make theirs a beautiful one.
